I noticed that uninstalling the Trae IDE sometimes leaves behind configuration folders, extensions, and registry keys. I created a simple batch script to handle the cleanup process automatically.

**Features:**
*   **Dual Version Support:** Can clean Trae International and Trae CN independently.
